Quiver Mutation Database

Open research infrastructure for quivers, exchange matrices, and mutation-equivalence classes.

Status: Under active development

QMD will provide a curated, citable, and reproducible database of quivers and mutation classes, together with rigorously defined invariants and transparent computational provenance. See the About page for more information.
